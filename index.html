About Me

My name is Ayush Singh and I am a PhD student in the Geometry and Mathematical Physics group of the Mathematics Area at Scuola Internazionale Superiore di Studi Avanzati (SISSA), Trieste, Italy. Before SISSA, I did a master's in physics from National Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhubaneswar, India.

I like abstract math, its applications to physics, and I write code in my free time. I also blog sometimes.
